What does it mean to give someone a tip?

If you tip someone such as a waiter in a restaurant,you give them some money to thank them for their services.


How to calculate a tip:

tip amount = bill amount × tip percentage. Thus, the tip amount is equal to the total bill amount times the tip percentage in decimal form.


How Much is a Good Tip?

There are many different opinions on what a good tip is, and ultimately it’s up to you how much to tip for the service you receive. It’s generally agreed that 15% is a good starting point for a tip.
